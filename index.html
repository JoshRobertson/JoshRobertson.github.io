About Me

Hi, I'm Josh

I'm not so much a developer as I am a builder. I'm not defined by my tools. Whether its code, a whiteboard and marker, some Post-its, or just some Lego blocks, I'm driven by a love of building systems.

When I'm not building things, you can usually find me out running, trying every craft beer under the sun, or reading comic books.
